Island Invasion

  • Role: Developer/ producer
  • Platforms: Steam
  • Where and when: Curveball Games - March 18 to Dec 18 (10 months)
  • Key responsibilities:
    • Developed my own game for the Steam store. Island Invasion was a tower defense game with procedurally generated maps and unlimited waves of enemies.
    • The player can build on land or sea, depending on building type. They must manage their cashflow with rewards from enemies as well as mining oil, which they can then purchase new towers with or upgrade existing towers.
    • Multiple tower types: mortar (splash damage), gun, laser (more damage the longer it's on an enemy), temporal (slows enemies down), flak (splash airborne damage), and missile (land or air homing missiles).
    • Towers can target first, last, strong or weak enemies, a QoL improvement that I had positive feedback on.
    • Implemented leaderboards and achievements with Steamworks.
    • Wrote all code, developed 3D assets, and managed release of the game to Steam.
    • Worked with two contractors, a 2D artist and a composer, who supported on areas I'm not skilled at.
    • Removed from Steam as it wasn't worth keeping up due to the £100 HMRC bill every year!
